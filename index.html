About Me
I am a 3rd year PhD candidate at Brown University in the Encrypted Systems Lab where I'm advised by Prof. Seny Kamara. In the summer of 2021, I was selected as a Research Fellow at ArthurAI. Previously, I received a B.S. in mathematics and computer science from the University of Maryland - College Park where I worked with John Dickerson. My research broadly examines robustness in machine learning, with a particular focus on questions regarding algorithmic fairness and the design of audits for black box algorithms adversarial environments.
